What is CodeAir?

CodeAir 1.0 is a coding competition that is based on providing this opportunity to the developers to come forward and show their skills. It is also a platform for the companies to hand pick the right talent as per their needs.

How will CodeAir work?

In CodeAir 1.0, the students will solve problems in a limited time frame (in this case, 5 hours). This constraint will help them be more disciplined, fast and efficient – the key qualities to becoming a better coder. Given the time constraint, they'll also look at problems differently, which will introduce them to new approaches and ways to solve a problem.

What stands out?

The factors that would help the competition standout in terms of value are the provision of practical work experience, an opportunity to get mentorship/training from well recognized tech companies, certification from recognized tech communities, and coding career market analysis from the lens of a local industry designer.

F.A.Q's

  • What services do you offer?

    We provide training and sessions on the latest technologies such as Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Low Code Development, Web Development, and much more. This further extends to even career-based sessions where we invite trainers and industry experts from across the globe.

  • Are you limited to technology only?

    We are a diverse community focused on empowering the masses. Therefore, we focus on non-technical events as well that shape students into the next impact makers.

  • Is this community supported by Microsoft?

    Yes, as this community comes within the Microsoft Learn Student Ambassador program the community consists of verified MLSA’s that are overseen by Microsoft Community Program Managers.

  • How can I be a part of this community?

    You can scroll down to "Wish To Join MLSA-Air University" and click on Register. You will be directed to a Microsoft Form where you can provide your details and register your interest in joining our community.

  • Is this community limited to students only?

    Yes, you must be a student from an accredited university.

  • Can non-technical students join MLSA-Air University?

    As we focus on encouraging more students into technology and innovation, it truly does not matter what your discipline is. Hence, students from all respective fields are welcomed. However, we encourage some minor knowledge about technology.
